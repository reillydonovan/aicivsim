The dividend is funded by five streams, modeled after Alaska's Permanent Fund but powered by digital infrastructure:

<div class="steps">
  <div class="step">
    <h4>AI compute rents (35%)</h4>
    <p>Levied on data center operators using local grid and land resources. $875M/year.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Green tariffs (25%)</h4>
    <p>Carbon-adjusted import tariffs recycled into the dividend pool. $625M/year.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Data commons licensing (20%)</h4>
    <p>Revenue from anonymized public data access licensed to researchers and firms. $500M/year.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Philanthropy + federal match (15%)</h4>
    <p>Matched grants from mission-aligned foundations and federal programs. $375M/year.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Cooperative revenue share (5%)</h4>
    <p>Surplus from publicly-owned cooperative AI agents and automation platforms. $125M/year.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Resident journeys: how it works

<div class="callout callout--tip">
  <p>Each journey in CivilizationOS is designed to survive offline and low-connectivity environments. Context follows the resident across every touchpoint via W3C DID-based identity â€” no PII leakage.</p>
</div>

Take the **Join** journey as an example. A new resident:

<div class="steps">
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Receives invitation or self-registers</h4>
    <p>Via web portal, SMS, community hub walk-in, or IVR phone flow.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Verifies identity</h4>
    <p>W3C DID-based â€” proves residency without leaking personal information.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Signs consent packages</h4>
    <p>Reviews what data is collected, how it's used, and selects communication preferences.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Creates resident profile</h4>
    <p>Skills, interests, household info. This feeds into Transition OS matching and benefit eligibility.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Receives welcome kit + first Civic Dividend</h4>
    <p>Disbursed via digital wallet with ACH fallback. Average onboarding: 7 days. 89% completion rate.</p>
  </div>
</div>
