## AI audit coverage: 10% to 100%

<div class="callout callout--warning">
  <p>In 2026, only 10% of high-risk AI systems have participatory safety reviews. The charter mandates 100% coverage by 2035, with threshold breaches triggering automatic policy reviews, funding reallocations, or temporary pauses.</p>
</div>

The audit tracker models this trajectory year by year, showing:

<div class="steps">
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Coverage percentage</h4>
    <p>From 10% (2026) to 100% (2035) â€” a 10x expansion of charter-mandated participatory reviews.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Incident tracking</h4>
    <p>Incidents rise initially as more systems are reviewed (peaking at 15 in 2029), then decline as compliance matures to just 2 by 2035.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="step">
    <h4>Resolution rates</h4>
    <p>Consistently above 90%, reaching 100% resolution by 2030 as processes mature.</p>
