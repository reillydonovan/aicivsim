01

What It Is

A real-time 3D network graph that maps the six civilizational operating systems — their health, their interdependencies, and their response to four divergent policy futures.

The AI Civilization Simulator models six domains: climate, governance, workforce transition, civilization health, simulation engine, and strategy. Each page on this site explores one domain in depth. The Visualizer renders all six simultaneously as an interconnected 3D network — a single view of the entire system.

Nodes orbit a central Civilization Index. Connections between them carry weighted influence — a governance failure propagates differently than a climate tipping point. The visualization makes these dynamics tangible.

03

How It Works

3D Network

Six system nodes orbit a central composite index. Each node’s size, spin speed, color saturation, and opacity are driven by its health score. Connections between nodes carry directional, weighted influence — brighter and thicker when the link is strong, faded when weak.

Cosmic Symphony

Each system has a unique audio voice tuned to a note in a Cmaj9 chord. When healthy, the voices synchronize into a consonant, harmonized soundscape. As systems deteriorate, vibrato increases, pitch drifts, and rhythmic pulses desynchronize — an auditory signal that the system is fracturing.

Smooth Transitions

All visual and audio changes use exponential easing. Switching scenarios doesn’t snap — it morphs. Node scales, colors, bloom intensity, fog, rotation speed, and audio parameters all interpolate continuously toward their targets.

Data-Driven

The visualizer pulls its metrics and timeseries data from the same shared data layer that powers every dashboard on this site. Update a scenario projection in the data source and the 3D view, charts, and world-state narratives all reflect the change automatically.

Controls Reference

Mouse / Touch

Click a node — fly the camera in and open the system detail panel with charts, scores, and override controls.

Click again — deselect and fly back to overview.

Double-click a node — navigate to its full dashboard page.

Drag — orbit the camera freely around the scene.

Scroll — zoom in and out.

Keyboard Shortcuts

1 – 4 — switch scenario (Aggressive, Moderate, BAU, Worst).

M — toggle sound on/off.

Esc — deselect node and return to overview.

Left / Right arrows — step through the timeline year by year.
