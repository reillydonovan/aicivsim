About the Advisor

An AI assistant trained on the full data model behind AICIVSIM — 7 systems, 4 scenarios, 42 cross-system feedback loops, and 50 years of projections. Ask anything.

How It Works

The advisor understands every metric, scenario, tipping point, and policy lever modeled on this site. Ask it questions in natural language and it responds with data-rich answers, linking you to the relevant dashboards and pages.

The chat widget persists in the bottom-right corner of every page. Navigate the site while keeping your conversation going — click any page link in a response to jump there without losing context.

Setup

Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner, then open settings (gear icon) to paste your API key. Keys are stored in your browser's localStorage only — never sent anywhere except your chosen provider (OpenAI or Anthropic).

Supported models: GPT-4o, GPT-4o Mini, Claude Sonnet, Claude Haiku. Responses stream in real-time.
